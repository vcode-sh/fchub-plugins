## Before You Start

You need three things:

- **WordPress 6.7** or newer
- **PHP 8.3** or newer (ask your host if unsure — they love that question)
- **FluentCart** installed and activated

That's it. If FluentCart isn't active when you activate Multi-Currency, the plugin will politely tell you and wait. No errors, no drama, just a quiet admin notice.

<Callout type="info" title="Optional Extras">
**FluentCRM** and **FluentCommunity** aren't required, but if you have them installed, Multi-Currency will automatically sync currency preferences to contacts and community profiles. Zero extra setup.
</Callout>

## Install the Plugin

Two minutes, tops. Pick whichever method you prefer:

<Tabs items={['Upload via WordPress Admin', 'Upload via FTP']}>
<Tab value="Upload via WordPress Admin">

The easy way. No FTP clients, no file managers, no existential dread.

<Steps>
  <Step>
    **Download the ZIP**

    Grab the latest <PluginDownload plugin="fchub-multi-currency">fchub-multi-currency.zip</PluginDownload> from the releases page.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Upload it**

    In your WordPress admin, go to **Plugins → Add New → Upload Plugin**. Choose the ZIP file, click **Install Now**.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Activate**

    Click **Activate Plugin**. Done. The plugin sets itself up automatically — you'll see "Multi-Currency" appear in your FluentCart settings within seconds.
  </Step>
</Steps>

</Tab>
<Tab value="Upload via FTP">

For those who enjoy the classics.

<Steps>
  <Step>
    **Extract the ZIP**

    Unzip `fchub-multi-currency.zip` on your computer. You'll get a folder called `fchub-multi-currency`.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Upload the folder**

    Upload the entire `fchub-multi-currency` folder to `/wp-content/plugins/` on your server.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Activate in WordPress**

    Go to **Plugins** in your WordPress admin, find **FCHub - Multi-Currency**, click **Activate**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

</Tab>
</Tabs>

## Check That It's Working

After activation, head to **FluentCart → Settings → Multi-Currency**. You should see the settings panel with six tabs. If you see it, everything's working.

<Callout type="info" title="Direct Link">
Bookmark this: `/wp-admin/admin.php?page=fluent-cart#/settings/multi-currency`
</Callout>

Want a quick health check? Click the **Diagnostics** tab — it shows your PHP version, plugin version, and whether all the pieces are connected properly.

<Separator className="my-8" />

## What's Next

The plugin ships with sensible defaults, but it won't do much until you tell it which currencies to show. Head to [Configuration](/docs/fchub-multi-currency/configuration) and:

1. Set your base currency (if it's not USD)
2. Add the currencies your customers care about
3. Pick an exchange rate provider (or use the free ECB option)
4. Add the **Currency Switcher** block to your layout, or drop the `[fchub_currency_switcher]` shortcode on classic/theme areas

Five minutes of setup, and your store speaks every currency that matters.

## Removing the Plugin

**Deactivating** pauses everything but keeps your data safe. Reactivate any time — your currencies, rates, and customer preferences are all still there.

**Deleting** the plugin? By default, your data stays put even after deletion. If you want a clean break, flip the "Remove all data on uninstall" toggle in settings *before* you delete. That wipes everything — rate history, settings, saved preferences, the lot.

<Callout type="warn" title="Data Removal Is Opt-In">
We don't delete your data unless you explicitly ask for it. Because surprises are for birthdays, not plugin management.
</Callout>
