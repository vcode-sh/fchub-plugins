## Before You Start

You need three things:

- **WordPress 6.7** or newer
- **PHP 8.3** or newer
- **FluentCart** installed and activated

That's it. If FluentCart isn't active when you activate FCHub Wishlist, the plugin will tell you and wait. No errors, no drama, just a quiet admin notice.

<Callout type="info" title="Optional Extras">
**FluentCRM** isn't required, but if you have it installed, Wishlist will automatically tag contacts based on their wishlist activity. **FCHub Multi-Currency** will convert wishlist prices automatically. Zero extra setup for either.
</Callout>

## Install the Plugin

<Tabs items={['Upload via WordPress Admin', 'Upload via FTP']}>
<Tab value="Upload via WordPress Admin">

The easy way. No FTP clients, no file managers, no existential dread.

<Steps>
  <Step>
    **Download the ZIP**

    Grab the latest <PluginDownload plugin="fchub-wishlist">fchub-wishlist.zip</PluginDownload> from the releases page.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Upload it**

    In your WordPress admin, go to **Plugins > Add New > Upload Plugin**. Choose the ZIP file, click **Install Now**.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Activate**

    Click **Activate Plugin**. Done. You'll see "Wishlist" appear in your FluentCart settings within seconds.
  </Step>
</Steps>

</Tab>
<Tab value="Upload via FTP">

For those who enjoy the classics.

<Steps>
  <Step>
    **Extract the ZIP**

    Unzip `fchub-wishlist.zip` on your computer. You'll get a folder called `fchub-wishlist`.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Upload the folder**

    Upload the entire `fchub-wishlist` folder to `/wp-content/plugins/` on your server.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Activate in WordPress**

    Go to **Plugins** in your WordPress admin, find **FCHub - Wishlist**, click **Activate**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

</Tab>
</Tabs>

## Check That It's Working

After activation, head to **FluentCart > Settings > Wishlist**. You should see the settings panel with four tabs: General, Display, FluentCRM, and Statistics. If you see them, everything's working.

<Callout type="info" title="Direct Link">
Bookmark this: `/wp-admin/admin.php?page=fluent-cart#/settings/wishlist`
</Callout>

Visit your shop page — you should see heart icons on every product card. If hearts are showing, you're done.

<Separator className="my-8" />

## What's Next

The plugin ships with sensible defaults and works out of the box. But you might want to tweak a few things:

1. Enable or disable guest wishlists
2. Customise the icon style and button text
3. Turn on FluentCRM tag sync

Head to [Configuration](/docs/fchub-wishlist/configuration) for the full rundown.

<Separator className="my-8" />

## Removing the Plugin

**Deactivating** pauses everything but keeps your data safe. Reactivate any time — wishlists, settings, and statistics are all still there.

**Deleting** the plugin? By default, your data stays put even after deletion. If you want a clean break, flip the "Remove Data on Uninstall" toggle in settings *before* you delete. That wipes everything — wishlist items, settings, statistics, the lot.

<Callout type="warn" title="Data Removal Is Opt-In">
We don't delete your data unless you explicitly ask for it. Because surprises are for birthdays, not plugin management.
</Callout>
