FCHub Wishlist adds heart icons to your product cards, a dedicated wishlist page in the customer's account, guest wishlist support via cookies, and FluentCRM tag sync — all without a single line of custom code.

## Why Bother?

A visitor browses your store, sees something they like, but isn't ready to buy. Without a wishlist, that product disappears the moment they close the tab. With a wishlist, they click a heart, come back next week, and buy it. Or they don't. But at least they had the option.

More practically: wishlist data tells you which products people *want* but haven't bought yet. That's marketing gold. Pair it with FluentCRM and you can send "still thinking about it?" emails to people who wishlisted a product three days ago.

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step>
    **Visitor sees a product they like**

    A heart icon appears on every product card in the shop. On the single product page, there's a dedicated "Add to Wishlist" button. Both do the same thing.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **One click to save**

    Click the heart — it turns red. The product is saved to the wishlist. If the visitor is logged in, it's stored in the database. If they're a guest, it goes into a cookie.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Wishlist lives in My Account**

    Logged-in customers can see all their saved items in the "My Wishlist" tab of the FluentCart customer portal. Each item shows the product name, variant, price, and "Add to Cart" button.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Bulk add to cart**

    The "Add All to Cart" button does exactly what it says. One click, entire wishlist in the cart. Items are optionally auto-removed from the wishlist after purchase.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **You get the data**

    The statistics tab shows which products are most wishlisted. FluentCRM tags contacts automatically. You know what people want — now sell it to them.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Requirements

| Requirement | Version |
|---|---|
| WordPress | 6.7+ |
| PHP | 8.3+ |
| FluentCart | Latest version |

<Callout type="warn" title="FluentCart Required">
This is a FluentCart extension. Without FluentCart, the plugin politely does nothing.
</Callout>

**Optional extras** — not required, but nice to have:

| Plugin | What It Adds |
|---|---|
| **FluentCRM** | Automatic wishlist activity tags on contacts |
| **FCHub Multi-Currency** | Wishlist prices convert automatically when visitors switch currency |
