## General

The on/off switches and core behaviour. Start here.

![General tab with wishlist master switch, guest support, auto-remove, and cleanup settings](/wishlists/fluentcart-wishlists-general.webp)

### Wishlist Enabled

The master switch. **Enabled** turns on heart icons, the My Account tab, everything. **Disabled** hides it all but keeps your data safe.

### Guest Wishlist

Allow non-logged-in visitors to use wishlists via cookies. Guests can browse, add items, and come back later — their wishlist survives as long as the cookie does. If they register or log in, the guest wishlist merges with their account automatically.

### Auto-remove Purchased Items

When enabled, items are automatically removed from the wishlist after the customer buys them. Clean and tidy — no "already purchased" clutter.

### Max Items per Wishlist

Hard cap on how many items a single wishlist can hold. Default is **50**. Set it lower if you want to keep things focused, higher if your customers are ambitious hoarders.

### Guest Cleanup (days)

Guest wishlists older than this get removed by the daily cleanup cron job. Default is **30 days**. Keeps your database lean without deleting active guests too aggressively.

### Remove Data on Uninstall

Leave this on **No (keep data)** unless you're absolutely sure you're done with the plugin. When set to **Yes (delete all)**, removing the plugin wipes everything — wishlist items, settings, statistics. There's no undo.

## Display

Controls how the wishlist buttons appear on your storefront.

![Display tab with icon style, button text, and counter badge settings](/wishlists/fluentcart-wishlists-display.webp)

### Show Heart on Product Cards

Adds the wishlist heart overlay to product cards in the shop grid and list views. When enabled, every product card gets a clickable heart icon in the top-right corner.

### Show Button on Single Product

Adds an "Add to Wishlist" button on single product pages. Shows below the "Add to Cart" button.

### Icon Style

Choose between a **Heart** or a different icon style for the wishlist button. Hearts are the convention — people know what they mean.

### Button Text (Add) / Button Text (Remove)

The text shown on the single product page button. Defaults to "Add to Wishlist" and "Remove from Wishlist". Customise to match your store's tone.

### Counter Badge in Header

Show a wishlist item count badge via the `[fchub_wishlist_count]` shortcode or `wp_footer` hook. Visitors see a small number next to the wishlist icon in your header — like a mini cart counter, but for wishes.

## FluentCRM

Connects wishlist activity to FluentCRM tagging. Only matters if you have FluentCRM installed.

![FluentCRM tab with tag sync, prefix, and auto-create settings](/wishlists/fluentcart-wishlists-fluentcrm.webp)

### FluentCRM Tag Sync

When enabled, tags are automatically applied and removed in FluentCRM based on wishlist activity. A customer who adds a product to their wishlist gets a tag. Remove the item, tag goes away.

### Tag Prefix

Prefix for auto-created tags. Default is `wishlist:`. So a customer who wishlists a product called "Classic Hoodie" might get a tag like `wishlist:active`.

### Auto-create Tags

Automatically creates tags in FluentCRM if they don't already exist. Leave this on unless you want to manage tags manually.

See [FluentCRM Integration](/docs/fchub-wishlist/fluentcrm) for the full breakdown of what syncs and when.
