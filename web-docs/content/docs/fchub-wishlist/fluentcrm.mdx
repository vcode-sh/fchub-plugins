If you're running FluentCRM, FCHub Wishlist can automatically tag contacts based on their wishlist activity. No manual work, no CSV imports, no "I wonder what products people are interested in" guessing.

## How It Works

When a customer adds a product to their wishlist, the plugin applies a tag to their FluentCRM contact. Remove the item, the tag is removed. It's real-time — no cron delays, no batch processing.

![FluentCRM settings tab with tag sync, prefix, and auto-create options](/wishlists/fluentcart-wishlists-fluentcrm.webp)

### What Gets Tagged

| Event | Tag Applied | Tag Removed |
|---|---|---|
| Customer adds item to wishlist | `wishlist:active` | — |
| Customer removes item from wishlist | — | If wishlist is now empty |
| Customer purchases wishlisted item | — | If auto-remove is enabled |

The tag prefix is configurable — default is `wishlist:`. Change it to whatever fits your existing tag taxonomy.

## Setup

1. Make sure **FluentCRM** is installed and activated
2. Go to **FluentCart > Settings > Wishlist > FluentCRM**
3. Enable **FluentCRM Tag Sync**
4. Leave **Auto-create Tags** on (unless you prefer to pre-create tags manually)
5. Save

That's it. Tags start flowing immediately.

## Automation Funnels

FCHub Wishlist registers its own trigger group in FluentCRM's automation builder. When creating a new funnel, select **FCHub Wishlist** from the trigger list to access two dedicated triggers:

![FluentCRM automation funnel builder showing FCHub Wishlist triggers — Item Added to Wishlist and Item Removed from Wishlist](/wishlists/fluentcart-wishlists-fluentcrm-funnel.webp)

| Trigger | Fires When |
|---|---|
| **Item Added to Wishlist** | A customer adds any product to their wishlist |
| **Item Removed from Wishlist** | A customer removes a product from their wishlist |

These are full automation triggers — you can chain any FluentCRM actions after them: send emails, apply tags, add to lists, set delays, add conditions, the works.

## What You Can Do With It

Once tags and automations are flowing, you can:

- **Segment by interest** — create a segment of everyone with a `wishlist:active` tag. These are people who want something but haven't bought it yet.
- **Trigger re-engagement** — set up an automation on the "Item Added to Wishlist" trigger with a 3-day delay. Send a "still thinking about it?" email.
- **Discount nudges** — combine wishlist tags with purchase history. Someone who wishlisted a product but hasn't bought after 7 days? Send them a coupon.
- **Product launch follow-ups** — when you update a product that many people have wishlisted, email them about the update.

<Separator className="my-8" />

## Requirements

- **FluentCRM** must be installed and activated
- The customer must have a FluentCRM contact (created manually or via any FluentCRM integration)
- Guest wishlists don't trigger CRM tags — the visitor needs to be a logged-in customer with a matching FluentCRM contact
